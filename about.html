Our Story

Who We Are

Jodalsam Global Limited is a dynamic Nigerian company at the forefront of construction excellence and energy distribution. With over 15 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for both public and private sector clients across Plateau State and beyond.

Founded on the principles of quality, integrity, and innovation, we deliver comprehensive construction solutions spanning planning & design, project management, procurement, and quality monitoring. Our expertise extends to the oil & gas sector, where we operate a growing network of service stations providing reliable petroleum products to communities throughout the region.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to excellence in every project we undertake. From building critical infrastructure that shapes our nation's landscape to ensuring consistent fuel supply through our service stations, we understand the vital role we play in Nigeria's development. Our team of over 500 skilled professionals brings together local knowledge and international best practices to deliver projects that stand the test of time.

We take pride in our track record of completing over 200 projects successfully, always prioritizing safety, quality, and client satisfaction. Whether it's a complex construction project or maintaining the highest standards at our 12+ service stations, we bring the same dedication and professionalism to everything we do.
